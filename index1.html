About Me

I am a geologist and PhD candidate researching carbon capture and storage at the National Oceanographic Centre, University of Southampton, UK. My work focuses on developing innovative solutions for achieving net-zero carbon emissions and mitigating climate change impacts.

Research

By leveraging cutting-edge technology and scientific methodologies, I strive to contribute to global efforts in achieving a sustainable and environmentally friendly future.
